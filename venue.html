Conference Venue

CWI Building

Join us at the Conference Centre next to the CWI Building! The Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) is the national research institute for mathematics and computer science in the Netherlands. Renowned as the birthplace of Python, this iconic venue is a nexus of scientific progress and innovation. Whether you're here to share knowledge or simply soak in the atmosphere of discovery, the location promises to make this event memorable.

How to reach CWI

CWI is located at Science Park 123, 1098 XG Amsterdam. The street name 'Science Park' has been in use since 2011. In case your navigation system would not recognize it, please use the old address 'Kruislaan 413, 1098 SJ Amsterdam' instead.

CWI can be easily reached by public transport. CWI is a five minute walk away from railway station Amsterdam Sciencepark. For up-to-date details on your journey, you can visit the Nederlandse Spoorwegen (NS) website or the OV9292 website, the central source of information for public transport in Holland.
