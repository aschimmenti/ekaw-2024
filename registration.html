Registration

The Conference

Welcome to the registration page for the 24th International Conference on Knowledge Engineering and Knowledge Management (EKAW-24). The conference will be held in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, at the Amsterdam Science Park campus from November 26th to November 28th, 2024. We are excited to gather scholars, researchers, and practitioners from around the world for high-quality research presentations, stimulating discussions, and valuable networking opportunities.

The registration fees are as follows:

  • Student early bird: €250 (until 17/10)
  • Student late registration: €350 (until 19/11)

  • Regular early bird: €550 (until 17/10)
  • Regular late registration: €650 (until 19/11)

Please note that each accepted paper in the main conference requires at least one author to register at the regular fee. The maximum capacity for the conference center is 150 participants. Additionally, since the restaurant where the social dinner will take place has a maximum capacity, dinner tickets will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Therefore, we encourage you to register early to secure your spot at the dinner!

For registration, please use the following form: EKAW 2024 Registration Form. The registration fee covers conference halls and equipment, lunches, coffee breaks, a social event, a social dinner, keynote speakers, and awards. We look forward to your participation in EKAW-24 and to welcoming you to Amsterdam!
